LinearRegression
===========

## 基本形式
$$
	f(x)=\omega ^{T}x+b
$$

## 简单线性回归 Simple Linear Regression

> y has to be explained/forecasted on the basis of one single independent variable, x.(y and x both are interval variables)

$$
        f(x)=\omega ^{T}x+b 使得 f(x_{i})\cong y_{i}
$$
